LA Trip!

⯎ Santa Monica ⯎

Santa Monica is a coastal city in California, situated west of Los Angeles. Known for its beautiful beach, Santa Monica Beach, it's a popular destination for tourists and locals alike. The city features the famous Santa Monica Pier with its iconic Ferris wheel, arcade, and various amusements.

The landscape and mood was really nice.
Also watching the sunset at beach was unforgettable. The food was really good.

⯎ Hollywood Sign ⯎

The Hollywood Sign is an iconic landmark located in the hills of Los Angeles, California. It's situated on Mount Lee in the Hollywood Hills area and overlooks the Hollywood district.

It was really cool to see hollywood sign for real.
I take a lot of pictures there.

⯎ The Getty ⯎

The Getty Center, situated atop a hill in the Santa Monica Mountains, offers breathtaking architecture, stunning gardens, and a vast collection of art, including European paintings, drawings, sculptures, decorative arts, and photographs.

There was lot of famous art in the Getty.
The garden was so nice.

⯎ Griffith Observatory ⯎

The Griffith Observatory is an iconic and historic landmark located in Los Angeles, California. Situated on the southern slope of Mount Hollywood in Griffith Park, it offers stunning views of the city and the surrounding area.

The nightscape was really nice.
I watched the planetarium and it was really nice experience.

⯎ Universal Studio ⯎

Universal Studios Hollywood is a renowned film studio and theme park located in the San Fernando Valley area of Los Angeles, California. It's one of the oldest and most famous Hollywood film studios and offers visitors an immersive entertainment experience.

I played at the theme park for a whole day and take a lot of ride!
My favorite was Harrypotter ride.
